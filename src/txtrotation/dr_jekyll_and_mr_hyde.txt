I stood already committed to a profound duplicity of life.  Many a man would have even blushed to confess the secret of his feelings.  The power was within me to turn my face to the rigid laws of honour and rectify my conduct, or to drink deeper draughts of madness and become a stranger to myself.  It was not so much that I knew of any immediate evil, but that the impulse to pursue the darker path overwhelmed my reason.
