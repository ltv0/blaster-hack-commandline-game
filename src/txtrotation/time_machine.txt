The Time Traveller (for so it will be convenient to speak of him) was expounding a recondite matter to us.  His gray eyes shone, and there came a strange light over his face which made me think of one of those anxious men in Dickens, who are always on the edge of some revelation.  He was in the act of unfolding a theory that defied the common notions of space and time, and his eagerness made the firelight quiver on the walls.
