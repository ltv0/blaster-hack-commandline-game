No one would have believed in the last years of the nineteenth century that this world was being watched keenly and closely by intelligences greater than man's, and yet as mortal as his own.  Yet across the gulf of space, minds immeasurably superior to ours regarded this earth with envious eyes, and slowly and surely drew their plans against us.  The more I consider the matter, the more I am convinced that the story I have to tell is not of a dream but of a portent that once became a ghastly reality.
